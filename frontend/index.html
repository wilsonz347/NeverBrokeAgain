About Us

Our project promotes financial inclusion by addressing a critical challenge international students face in the U.S.: navigating the student loan process. Many international students struggle to: • Estimate the loan amount they need. • Manage their repayment plans effectively. To solve this, we are developing an innovative app that helps international students: • Calculate the optimal loan amount. • Create a clear, manageable repayment schedule. The app simplifies financial planning by: • Guiding students through estimating their expenses, including tuition, living costs, and other variables. • Generating personalized loan repayment schedules. • Empowering students to make informed financial decisions that align with their specific needs. In the U.S. financial system, student loans play a crucial role in enabling access to education, but navigating different loan types, interest rates, and repayment options can be overwhelming, especially for international students. Our app bridges this gap by: • Offering an easy-to-use tool that simplifies the complexities of student loans. • Helping students plan their finances with confidence. • Reducing the financial stress associated with higher education.
